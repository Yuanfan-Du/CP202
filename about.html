Who Are we?

Eat Time - Our goal is to provide best online food ordering and delivery service in Waterloo region.

Through our network, restaurants gain more business, food couriers earn extra income on their own schedule, and busy customers are able to gain time by ordering from hundreds of local restaurants and national chains that never offered delivery before. Eat time is advancing the future of food delivery.
